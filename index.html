About Me
Hi, I'm Katrina!
I am a Canadian illustrator with a passion for creativity and visual story-telling. I am particularly drawn to creating illustrations that spark imagination and those that transport viewers to adventurous and fantastical worlds. Although my roots are in using pencils and pens, these days I mostly draw digitally. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio. I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoyed creating it.
